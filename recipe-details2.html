Afghani Bolani Recipe

  • Prep Time: 30 mins
  • Cook Time: 1 hour
  • Servings: 4–6

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati rice (soaked for 30 mins)
  • 500g lamb or beef (cut into chunks)
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 carrots (julienned)
  • ½ cup raisins
  • ½ cup slivered almonds or pistachios (optional)
  • 4–5 green cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp sugar (for caramelizing)
  • 4 cups water
  • 4 tbsp cooking oil or ghee

Instructions:

Cook the Meat:

Heat 2 tbsp oil in a pot. Add sliced onions and cook until golden. Add meat, salt, pepper, cumin, cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon. Sauté for 5–10 minutes. Add water and simmer until the meat is tender (about 45 minutes). Remove meat and set aside. Strain and keep the broth.

Prepare the Rice:

In a separate pot, boil the soaked rice in salted water until it's about 80% cooked. Drain and set aside. Make the Carrot-Raisin Topping: In a small pan, heat 2 tbsp oil. Add sugar and let it caramelize lightly. Add julienned carrots, raisins, and nuts. Sauté for a few minutes until soft and slightly glossy.

Assemble the Pulao:

In a large pot, layer half of the rice, then the cooked meat, and finally the remaining rice. Pour the reserved broth (about 1½ cups) over the rice. Cover tightly and steam on low heat for 20–25 minutes.

Serve:

Gently fluff the rice with a fork. Top with the carrot-raisin mixture and serve warm with salad or chutney.
